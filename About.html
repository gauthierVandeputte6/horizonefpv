About

Welcome to HorizoneFPV, where innovation meets the skies!

Who We Are

HorizoneFPV was founded by a passionate drone enthusiast and engineering student with a vision to transform the way aerial footage is captured and experienced. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, immersive drone videos and services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Vision

At HorizoneFPV, we believe in pushing the boundaries of aerial technology. Our aim is to become the leading provider of drone services by combining cutting-edge technology with a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Whether it’s capturing breathtaking landscapes, documenting high-profile events, or providing valuable insights for industrial applications, we are dedicated to delivering unmatched aerial solutions.

Our Services

  • Aerial Photography & Videography: Capture stunning aerial views with our high-definition cameras, perfect for real estate, weddings, and promotional videos.
  • Event Coverage: Provide comprehensive coverage of events, giving a unique perspective that only drone footage can offer.
  • Industrial Inspection: Perform detailed inspections of infrastructure, such as buildings, bridges, and power lines, using advanced FPV drones equipped with high-resolution cameras.
  • Surveying & Mapping: Utilize our drones for precise surveying and mapping, ideal for construction projects, land assessments, and agricultural planning.
  • Customized Drone Solutions: We also offer tailor-made drone solutions to meet specific project requirements.

Our Technology

Our fleet of drones is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and top-notch performance. We continually invest in the latest advancements in drone technology to provide our clients with the best possible service.

Our Commitment

Quality and safety are at the forefront of everything we do. Our team of experienced pilots and technicians undergoes rigorous training and adheres to strict safety protocols to ensure that every project is executed flawlessly.

Meet the Founder

Gauthier’s dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction drives the company’s mission to deliver unparalleled drone services.
